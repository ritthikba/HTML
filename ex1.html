COCKROACH JANTA PARTY IS THE FUTURE OF POLITICS.

The Cockroach Janta Party (CJP; lit.'Cockroach People's Party') is an Indian satirical political movement.

The origin of the movement is tied to a contempt plea filed in Supreme Court of India against Delhi High Court for delay in designation of senior advocate title.
